Writer's Corner

Welcome to the Writer's Corner, a place to inspire and ignite your creativity. Here you'll find a new writing prompt each week, along with a space to share your work with our community.

Weekly Writing Prompts

Prompt #1: The Unspoken Message

You find an old, dusty bottle washed ashore. Inside is a rolled-up note, but there's no writing—just a cryptic drawing of a constellation and a single feather. What is the message, and who sent it? Write a short story or poem exploring the mystery.

Prompt #2: The Lost Art

A forgotten, ancient skill suddenly re-emerges in a world that has replaced human creativity with automation. What is this skill, and how does its rediscovery change society? Write a sci-fi or fantasy story about the tension between human ingenuity and technological efficiency.

Prompt #3: The Echo of a Memory

Write a personal essay or reflective piece about a specific place—a childhood home, a forgotten cafe, or a quiet park bench—and the single, powerful memory that is forever tied to it. Explore how a location can hold onto emotions long after the people have left.

Writer's Resources

A curated list of tools, communities, and learning resources to help you on your writing journey.

  • MasterClass: Writing Section

    Learn from some of the world's best writers and storytellers.

  • NaNoWriMo (National Novel Writing Month)

    A global community helping writers achieve their goals of writing a full novel in November.

  • Grammarly

    A free writing assistant that helps you check for grammar, spelling, and punctuation errors.

  • Thesaurus.com

    A helpful tool for finding synonyms and improving your vocabulary.

  • Goodreads

    Connect with other readers and writers, discover new books, and join writing groups.

  • Duotrope

    A service for finding literary magazines, journals, and agents to submit your work to.
